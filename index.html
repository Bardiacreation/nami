The House

A perfume is a memory you can wear. We chase the essence of a moment — the dust of an old room, smoke over a southern river, fog on a foreign street — and hold it still.

Founded in Tehran in 2018, NAMISANATI composes fragrance by hand in small batches, drawing on Persian raw materials and a long tradition of distillation. Each bottle is an extrait — concentrated, deliberate, made to last on the skin and in the memory.
